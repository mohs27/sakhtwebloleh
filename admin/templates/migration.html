Easily import all the data from your Automagick Tube Script version 1 to this version. Just enter the database info of version 1 and press the "Analyze Data" Button.

You will be able to choose what to import and what to discard. After that, proceed with the next step, and all data will be added, simple and fast.

If the database is not on the same server, then you should enter, in the "host" field, the IP or hostname of the database, keeping in mind that you should grant remote access to the user. If you don't know how to do that, contact your hosting support and request them to do it.
